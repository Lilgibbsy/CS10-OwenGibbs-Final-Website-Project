About

Nick Fury

Nick Fury is the creator of the avengers initiative. He also runs SHIELD (Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division). SHIELD is a secret organisation that's main function is to protect the world from above military grade threats. Nick Fury has no super powers, but is a very skilled spy.

Thor Odinson

Thor Odinson is the asgardian thunder god. He is the asgardian all-father Odin's son and god of mischief's brother. Thor is capable of controlling and harnessing the weather around him on all planets. Thor has two main weapons; mjolnir and stormbreaker. Mjolnir is so heavy only thor can lift it and it acts as a coduit for Thor's powers. Stormbreaker is a extremly powerful axe that allows thor to travel through space using the bifrost.

Captain America

Captain America is a modified human. During WWII the USA tested with the advancement of the human body. Steve Rogers a.k.a Captain America was one of the test subjects. The experiment was a success and Steve Rogers became a super soldier. At the end of WWII Captain America sacraficed himslef to save the USA and was frozen in ice, only to be awakened 70 years later.

Iron Man

Iron man has no special powers and is just a normal human. Even with only being a normal human, Tony Stark's genius and vast wealth allowed him to create a suit that simulates super powers. His suits allow him to fly, shoot laser beams, and locate targets with highly advanced vision.

The Hulk

The Hulk was once a human named Bruce Banner, but when a gamma radiation experiment malfunctioned with Bruce being in close proximity it change his DNA structure. Because of this Bruce Banner turns into the being called the Hulk whenever he is angry. In his Hulk form Bruce gains immense size, strenght, stamina, and agility. Along with his Hulk powers, Bruce Banner is a genius scientest.

Hawkeye

Hawkeye is a SHIELD operative who joined the avengers for his skill with a bow. Haweye's skills were personaly seledted by Nick Fury. Hawkeye has incredible eyesight and extremly fast reactions. Along with his bows skills he is an extremly capable close combat fighter.

Black Widow

Black Widow was a russian girl who was kidnapped by an organisation of assassins and was trained to kill from a very young age. She hated her life and felt immense guilt because of her past, so when Nick Fury offered her the job as an Avenger, she gladly accepted. She is a really capable fighter, spy and assassin.
